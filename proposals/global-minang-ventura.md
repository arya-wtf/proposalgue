## Project Background

Our client, PT Global Minang Ventura, aims to establish a strong digital presence that reflects their vision as a venture-backed brand bringing Minang cuisine to the global stage. The goal is to build a premium, multi-brand website that communicates credibility to international partners and investors, while creating a seamless brand experience across their two entities — Global Minang Ventura and Sederhana Global.

Elux Space will support this initiative by designing and developing a cohesive web ecosystem, delivering a complete digital platform that is ready to represent GMV in international markets.

---

## Project Scope of Work

Designing and developing the Global Minang Ventura web ecosystem to deliver a premium, multi-brand digital experience that supports partner acquisition, investor communication, and global brand expansion.

### A. Scope Details

This project focuses on building a complete, production-ready website that reflects GMV's premium positioning and multi-entity structure. The scope includes:

**UX Structure**
Organizing the website architecture and content flow across both brands to ensure clarity, navigational consistency, and a guided user journey from entry to inquiry.

**UI Design & Development**
Designing and building a responsive, premium website aligned with GMV's global brand positioning — including hero sections, storytelling layouts, and conversion-focused contact flows.

**Premium Visual Production**
Producing cinematic hero video, section background video, and curated premium visual assets to elevate the overall brand presentation across all key pages.

**Multi-Brand Integration**
Setting up redirect flow and shared visual language between globalminang.ventura and sederhana.global, ensuring both entities feel connected yet distinct.

**Quality Assurance**
Reviewing all pages and components for responsiveness, cross-device consistency, and production readiness prior to handover.
